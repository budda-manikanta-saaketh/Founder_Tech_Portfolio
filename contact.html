Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple MVP might take 2-3 months, while a full-featured platform could take 6-12 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your development process?

Our full-cycle development includes discovery & strategy, UI/UX design, development, testing, launch, and post-launch support. We handle everything from initial concept to market launch and ongoing maintenance.

Do you work with startups and small businesses?

Absolutely! We specialize in working with startups and small businesses. We understand the unique challenges and budget constraints of early-stage companies and offer flexible engagement models to fit your needs.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with modern, proven technologies including React, Next.js, Flutter, Node.js, Python, and cloud platforms like AWS. We choose the best tech stack based on your specific project requirements and goals.

How do you ensure project quality?

We follow industry best practices including code reviews, automated testing, performance optimization, and security audits. Our quality assurance process ensures your product is reliable, secure, and performs excellently.
